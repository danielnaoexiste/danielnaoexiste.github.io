Torrato is a grid-based maze exploration game made for Dev-U Jam, following the theme “It’s unbearably hot! (?)”. <br />

## About
Torrato is a game about a mouse trapped inside an oven! 
Save your friend and flee from the unbearable heat in this yummy maze game. <br />
It was made in 48 hours.

Following the theme “The game is a liar”, this exploration platformer lies about what items and powerups you got. <br />
CaveScape scored #37 in Fun and #141 overall, and was solo developed by me.<br />
It was made using Godot, Piskel, Audacity and Beepster.
