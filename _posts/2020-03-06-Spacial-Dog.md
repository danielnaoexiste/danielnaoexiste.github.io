Spacial Dog is a Ludum Dare 42 submission, following the theme “Running out of Space”. <br />

## About
A solar explosion destroyed your ship. Flee from Space, avoiding asteroids and the solar explosion while collecting fuel. <br />
Originally about an alpaca running out of Space, but the alpaca turned into a dog during development. <br />
Game made in 72 hours for Ludum Dare 42. Made with Unity, Piskel, Audacity and LMMS.
